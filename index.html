Who am I?

Hi! I'm Sam - a software developer based in Glasgow.

After completing my master's degree in Product Design Engineering, I decided to steer my career in a different direction. I took the plunge by signing up to CodeClan's intense 16 week software development course and pursued my love of programming - and all things computers.

I have now graduated CodeClan and also hold a level 8 PDA in Software Development. Combining my programming skill set with my innovation and interpersonal skills, I am a confident and adaptable software developer.

I am now looking for a role in which I can expand upon my current skills and experience and meet like-minded individuals!

I am always looking to learn more and meet new people so feel free to give me a message on my LinkedIn or email me at samgcassie@gmail.com.

Tech stack used

Animal Quiz

Tech stack used
JavaScript - Vue
MongoDB

Created a 10 question animal quiz for kids, with a bright and interactive interface. The questions are randomly created from a custom made database and points are awarded based on accuracy and time elapsed. At the end of the quiz, if the user places within the top 3 scores they will be placed on the high-score board.
